What is WebPitch?

WebPitch is a platform where you can connect with others to bring your ideas to life.
Whether you're looking for a team to work with, or you're looking for a project to work on, WebPitch is the place to be.


Getting started is simple - just create an account and you're off.
You can start pitching your ideas, or take a look at other people's ideas and help out if they spark your interest.

All completely free.
The two major sections
Collaborating
The process of working with others on a project. Find people to work with, or collaborate with someone you can help out. Not only can WebPitch help you get your idea heard, but it can also help you find others to work with.
Pitching
Where it all starts. Every idea starts with a seed - a small idea that can grow into something great.
Once you have your idea, let world to see it. Pitch your ideas globally through WebPitch and see what others think - chances are you'll strike gold.
Every idea has to start somewhere. WebPitch is the place to begin, and finish your idea.
Open to everyone, WebPitch is where you can share and explore new opportunities, and collaborate with others easily.
